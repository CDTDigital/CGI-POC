MyCAlerts.com is an alert service for California residents which monitors emergency events, such as fires, floods, and weather events. We also inform registered users of emergency and non-emergency events near them, via email, text message, or push notification. Register here to sign up for alerts!

Note that MyCAlerts is in its infancy, and hasn't yet implemented all emergency alerts. We will inform our users as we add more event types.
